Image Prompting med Nano Banana

Sex prompt-cluster att testa live. Varje tema har en exempelprompt, en mallprompt att kopiera och 2–3 extra varianter att köra direkt.

Starta passet Se formatet

Format

Så fungerar varje tema

Steg 1

Exempelprompt + bild

Ett referensresultat — se vad prompten ger för output-nivå innan du testar.

Steg 2

Mallprompt att kopiera

Template med variabler för stil, ljus, lins, komposition och mood. Kopiera och kör.

Steg 3

2–3 extra prompts

Korta variationer för snabb iteration och A/B-jämförelse inom temat.

Cluster 01

Porträtt: studio

Rent studiouttryck med tydlig ljussättning och kontrollerad bakgrund

Prompt:

Photorealistic studio portrait of [subject], 85mm lens, soft key light from left, subtle rim light, neutral gray seamless backdrop, cinematic skin texture, high detail, no text, no watermark.

Justera: subject, skin tone, light ratio, focal length.

↓ mall-prompt + extra

Mall-prompt

Kopiera och anpassa

Studio portrait of [person], [camera] lens, [key light setup], [fill light setup], [background], [mood], [color palette], ultra detailed, realistic, no text, no logo.

Extra prompts

High-key beauty portrait, white cyclorama, soft diffused light, editorial makeup, clean minimal feel.

Low-key dramatic portrait, pure black background, hard side light, high contrast shadows, intense gaze.

Fine art portrait, Rembrandt lighting, 50mm, shallow depth of field, desaturated warm tones, painterly texture.

Cluster 02

Porträtt: miljö

Miljöbaserade porträtt med narrativ, platskänsla och naturligt ljus

Prompt:

Environmental portrait of [subject] in [location], natural window light, candid stance, documentary mood, 35mm lens perspective, subtle grain, realistic color grading.

Justera: location, time of day, pose direction, narrative detail.

↓ mall-prompt + extra

Mall-prompt

Kopiera och anpassa

Environmental portrait of [person], in [scene], [lighting], [camera angle], [lens], [emotion], [color style], natural details, realistic texture, no text.

Extra prompts

Street portrait at golden hour, warm backlight, urban texture, spontaneous expression, shallow DoF.

Café portrait by rainy window, soft overcast light, intimate editorial framing, warm tones.

Workshop portrait in industrial loft, practical lamp pools, gritty documentary feel, 28mm wide.

Remote worker at kitchen table, morning side light, calm minimalist setting, coffee steam visible.

Cluster 06

Viralt

Hook-drivna prompts för social spridning med stark wow-effekt

Prompt:

Hyper-shareable social image of [concept], impossible visual twist, bold contrast, clean focal point, instantly understandable story, high-impact thumbnail readability, no text.

Justera: hook, exaggeration level, palette contrast, meme potential.

↓ mall-prompt + extra

Mall-prompt

Kopiera och anpassa

Create a viral social image of [idea], featuring [unexpected element], [camera style], [color strategy], clear emotional hook, platform-ready composition, no text.

Extra prompts

Tiny barista inside a giant coffee cup, cinematic steam, playful surreal realism, warm morning light.

City street frozen in time except one runner in motion, strong red accent, surreal contrast.

Split-scene before/after transformation in one frame, impossible but photorealistic lighting throughout.
